StreamVault

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13 May 2026  ·  Last updated: 13 May 2026

Short version. StreamVault is built to help you decide what to watch tonight. We do not collect, store, or sell personal information. Your watchlist and preferences stay on your device.

1. Who we are

StreamVault is a mobile application developed by Prime Quantum Studios, a solo indie studio based in London, United Kingdom. References to "we," "us," or "our" refer to Prime Quantum Studios.

2. Information we do not collect

StreamVault is designed to work without an account. We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers
  • Precise location, GPS data, or device identifiers
  • Browsing history outside the app
  • Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data

3. Information stored locally on your device

StreamVault saves the following information directly on your device using the operating system's secure local storage. This data never leaves your device:

  • Watchlist: titles you have saved
  • Watched list: titles you have marked as watched
  • Preferences: selected streaming region, language, haptic and notification toggles
  • Onboarding flag: a single value indicating whether you have completed the welcome flow

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

4. Third-party services

StreamVault uses the following third-party services to retrieve movie and TV information. We do not control how these providers operate. Please review their respective policies for details.

The Movie Database (TMDB)

Used for film and TV metadata, posters, backdrops, ratings, and streaming provider information. This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB. See: themoviedb.org/privacy-policy

Trakt.tv

Used for trending data, anticipated titles, and release calendars. See: trakt.tv/privacy

Fanart.tv

Used for high-resolution artwork. See: fanart.tv/privacy-policy

JustWatch (via TMDB)

Streaming availability data shown in the "Where to Watch" section is sourced from JustWatch through TMDB. See: justwatch.com/privacy-policy

5. Future services

We may introduce optional features that involve additional third-party services. When they are added, this policy will be updated to disclose them clearly before they are enabled. Currently planned future integrations include:

  • Advertising: if introduced, we plan to use Google AdMob. Standard ad SDK data such as advertising identifier may be processed by Google. You will be able to view and adjust ad personalization in your device settings.
  • Subscriptions: if introduced, an optional paid tier may be processed through Apple App Store, Google Play, or a payments platform such as RevenueCat. Payment information is handled entirely by the platform, never by StreamVault.

These features are not active at the time of writing.

6. Analytics and tracking

StreamVault does not include third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or behavioral profiling SDKs.

7. Children's privacy

StreamVault is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has somehow provided information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

8. International users

StreamVault is available globally. Because no personal data is collected, no cross-border data transfer occurs from our side. Third-party services listed above may process data in their own jurisdictions; please review their policies.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights such as access, deletion, or portability of personal data under regulations including the UK GDPR, EU GDPR, and California Consumer Privacy Act. Because we do not collect personal data, there is generally nothing to access or delete on our side. Locally stored data on your device can be deleted at any time by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
